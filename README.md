# Retail Sales Tableau Dashboard
## Project Overview

This project visualizes **retail sales performance** using sampled **M5 Accuracy dataset** across multiple stores and product categories using **Tableau**.
The goal is to explore how sales vary over **time, stores, departments, and events**, revealing hidden insights that can help optimize operations, inventory, and promotional strategies.

The dashboards starts with a **big-picture overview**, diving into **store-level performance**, and finally uncovering **seasonal and event-driven patterns** that shape retail behavior.

## Dashboard 1: Retail Overview

The Retail Overview dashboard provides a snapshot of the overall business performance, capturing how sales and item movement evolved over time across different categories and stores.

- The **Sales Over Time** chart highlights consistent growth trends, indicating strong sales momentum throughout the period. - The **Store by Sales** and **Category by Sales** visualizations reveal how different outlets and product lines contribute to total revenue.

In total, **total sales recorded** reached **228,884**, while **total items sold were 109,500**, emphasizing a healthy transaction volume and balanced category performance across the retail network.

This dashboard sets the foundation for deeper exploration by identifying overall trends and revenue distribution before drilling down into store-specific performance.

## Dashboard 2: Store Performance Overview

The Store Performance Overview dives deeper into how each store contributes to overall success, highlighting **top-performing outlets** and emerging growth opportunities.

- The **Yearly Comparison by Store** shows that **CA1** consistently leads in performance. Although CA1’s sales slightly dipped from **50,908** in **2014** to **48,287** in **2015**, it continues to dominate due to strong **food department sales**.
- Meanwhile, **TX1** shows encouraging growth, increasing from **33,837 to 36,709**, signaling improved demand and stable recovery.

- The **Top Performing Store** is **CA1**, generating total sales of **114,345**, contributing **43.34%** of overall revenue.

In the **Average Weekly Sales by Store**, **CA1** maintains steady sales around **944.7**, compared to 671 &563.3 for other stores — reflecting a **consistent and reliable weekly performance** that sustains **long-term profitability**.

The **Department-Wise Sales view** reinforces CA1’s dominance in the food department, underscoring its importance in driving higher customer engagement and repeat sales.

## Dashboard 3: Seasonality & Event Insights

The Seasonality and Event Insights dashboard reveals how **time-based patterns** and **special events** influence sales performance.

- **Monthly Trends** show clear seasonal peaks in **October and July**, where demand spikes noticeably.
- Sales also surge around major events like **Superbowl, Easter, and Thanksgiving**, reflecting strong event-driven consumer behavior.

- The **Highest Sales Recorded** reached **683**, while the **Average Daily Sales** across all stores is **313**, indicating steady performance throughout the year.

- Event analysis shows an **Event Sales Uplift** of **1.4%**, suggesting moderate event impact, while the **Weekend Sales Uplift** of **45.5%** highlights weekends as critical revenue periods.

- The **Weekly Sales Trend** further confirms **strong demand on Saturdays and Sundays**, aligning with customer shopping habits and leisure spending.

Together, these insights highlight actionable opportunities — such as **scheduling promotions during weekends** and **stocking high-demand categories in peak months** — to maximize profitability.

### Key Takeaways
- **CA1 Store** remains the overall leader, driven by exceptional food department sales.
- **TX1 Store** shows promising year-over-year growth, making it a **potential target for expansion**.
- **October & July** are peak months, while **Superbowl, Easter, and Thanksgiving** significantly influence buyer activity.
- **Weekends** drive nearly **45% higher sales**, making them key periods for promotional planning.

This project illustrates how **visual analytics can transform raw retail data into meaningful business insights**.
By combining **store-level analysis, time-based trends, and event-driven insights**, the dashboards provide a holistic view of retail dynamics — helping decision-makers focus on the factors that truly drive sales growth.
